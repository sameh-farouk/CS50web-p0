GET TO KNOW ME

Hi, my name is sameh farouk.

I'm a violinist, composer, music educator, and software developer.

I was born in 1982 in alqateef in The Kingdom of Saudi Arabia.

I came to Egypt in 1984 with my parents and brothers, and grew up mostly on Cairo in a musical family.

I got my B.A from the Higher Institute of Arabic Music in the Violin instrument specialization.

Later , i joined The National Arabic Music Ensemble, conducted by Selim Sahab @ cairo opera House.

Currently i'm working as a music educator @ the ministrey of education (MoE) in kuwait.
